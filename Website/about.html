hello, i am roflmaoist

actually, i am walker, the man behind roflmaoist and the person who coded this website all by himself (shocker)

roflmaoist is an electronic music project that began as a response to many things. one of them is the general loneliness and alienation endemic to post-industrial society. another is the role my own mental health issues have had in exacerbating that loneliness and alienation

another is a two-week trip to the united kingdom, manchester specifically. despite not being in "the big city," i was greeted with an open expression of artistic, political, and aesthetic radicalism that i had not yet seen in the united states (at least, not on the left). i was also taken by the many elements of overseas culture that borrow elements of american culture - uk electronic music regularly samples american soul singers and rappers, and i overheard a teenager in a record shop owner if he had "heard of playboi carti," calling him "a little devious" (imagine liam gallagher saying it, it sounded exactly like that)

my music is also a response to a very technological upbringing. I learned to be a voracious consumer through virtually uninhibited internet access in my teenage years (as many of us did). This led to a highly consumptive sense of identity - i am not defined by my believes or spiritual value, but instead by what i watch/listen to/read/purchase. i am not alone or novel in these observations - the internet's acceleration of our tendency to consume has been discussed by people a lot more intelligent than i am. but i don't think the role of music in this phenomenon has been explored sufficiently

there is a particular kind of person i imagine will like roflmaoist: the mentally ill but highly intelligent internet-savvy gen-zs/millenials with adderall scripts and enough esoteric knowledge in their head to fill a library. enough knowledge that they feel thoroughly alienated from the rest of functioning society. this might be projection though - only time will tell

thanks for reading this far. check out my music by pressing "listen" or hit me up with an email by pressing "contact"

ps: to make my political allegiances clear - i am leftist, but not a maoist. nazi punks, you know the rest
