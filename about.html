hello, i am roflmaoist

actually, i am walker, the man behind roflmaoist and the person who coded this website all by himself (shocker).

roflmaoist is an electronic music project that i started following a two week trip to the uk. i was greeted with an open expression of artistic, political, and aesthetic radicalism that i had not yet seen in the united states (at least, not on the left). i was also taken by the many elements of overseas culture that borrow elements of american culture - uk electronic music regularly samples american soul singers and rappers, and i overheard a young person in manchester asking a record shop owner if he had "heard of playboi carti," calling him "a little devious." roflmaoist also contains elements of j-music, although this isn't by design as much as it is by nostalgia. So, in an unintentional sense, roflmaoist is the channeling of non-american interpretations of american music back into the american sphere.

there is a particular kind of person i imagine will like roflmaoist: the mentally ill but highly intelligent internet-savvy gen-zs/millenials with adderall scripts and enough esoteric knowledge in their head to fill a library. enough knowledge that they feel thoroughly alienated from the rest of functioning society. this might be projection though - only time will tell.

thanks for reading this far. check out my music by pressing "listen" or hit me up with an email by pressing "contact."

ps: to make my political allegiances clear - i am a leftist, but not a maoist. i do tend to agree with mao more than marx, in the theoretical at least.
