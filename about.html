roflmaoist is an industrial electronic project by atlanta-based "artist" walker scott

roflmaoist exists in two forms:

roflmaoist (main) - live performances of original tracks. best for shows with bands. if you don't know what a band is, look up aerosmith

dj roflmaoist - aggressive dark techno sets with some originals thrown in for good measure. best for dancey events such as funerals

feel free to listen to my music or send me an email

ps: rofl is pronounced like roflcopter. maoist is just maoist. rofl-maoist. roflmaoist
